Our Engineering Services

Comprehensive structural engineering solutions tailored to meet the demands of modern construction projects.

01

Advanced Structural Modeling

3D Revit modeling and Finite Element Method analysis for complex models to ensure functional and structural reliability.

02

Structural Analysis & Design

Precise structural analysis and design calculations to ensure complete compliance with local codes and international standards.

03

Shop Drawings & Schedules

Detailed reinforcement drafting, schedules, and exact information on reinforcement drawings.

04

Steel Connection Design

Steel connection design to provide safe structural detailing and efficient operational solutions.

05

Wind Engineering & Studies

Evaluation of wind loads and structural response, including preliminary studies and interpretation of wind tunnel testing.

Structural design and analysis of signage structures including support frames, foundations, and anchorage systems.
